WHAT'S MY DESIGN PROCESS

01/ COLLABORATION



At the beginning of our journey together I will guide you through a series of informations to have a clear vision of the objectives of your brand or your site. After the first call, I will organise a discovery session that will be useful to define the work we will do together.

02/ STRATEGY



Strategy is the key ingredient for designing an unique and clear communication, whether it is your brand or your new website. We will work together through an exclusive process, thanks to which we will define the visual identity of your business and the communication strategy that will help you achieve your goals.

03/ DESIGN



Your brand is much more than just a logo! Together we will design all the elements that will define your services and the experience that your audience will have. During the design phase I will make you participate in each step, guiding you through the creation of your new brand or website.
