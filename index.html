WHAT'S MY DESIGN PROCESS

01/ DISCOVERY


After you get in touch, I will organise a discovery session that will be useful to define the work we will do together. We start our journey together with a conversation where we go through the goal of the project and specific wishes to see if my knowledge can generate value for your business or company.

02/ STRATEGY


To make your project the best it can be, I'll put together a strategy. With the goal in mind, we decide what content is needed in terms of text, images and features. We will work together through an exclusive process, thanks to which we will define the visual identity of your business and the communication strategy that will help you achieve your goals.

03/ DESIGN


With a clear strategy, I start designing. I'll deliver a great-looking design that enhances your business, always with the user in mind. During the design phase I will make you participate in each step, building solutions to put you in the driving seat of your brand or website's content!
