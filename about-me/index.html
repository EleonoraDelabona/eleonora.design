Hey, nice to meet you!



It would be a lie to tell you I've dreamed of doing this job since I was little.
I've always been a creative persone but I dreamed of being a doctor, an architect, a football player, a midwife...until few years ago I knew nothing about web design, HTML, user experience, wireframes, layouts!

I studied Architecture but once I graduated I didn't feel it was going to be MY job! So I started to work in a coffee shop and after some years as a manager I found my true passion: designing for the World Wide Web!

I’m currently living in Sardinia, working as a freelance web desinger and studying with Start2Impact to improve my UX/UI skills.
